Men Everywhere Are Finally Waking Up!

A new chapter of Man UnCivilized has opened up in Houston, TX and men are flocking to the meetings to learn about how to become "UnCivilized".

A new Men's group has been started in the Houston area. The group will help men in the area with Men's Work. Men's Work is a movement where men are working with other men to form groups of brotherhood. In these groups, men meet and talk about everything that isn't usually talked about. This is not a movement for small talk or surface level people. Man UnCivilized is a brotherhood of men that work together to grow and embrace the both their feminine and masculine sides.

Our mission: make sure that men have a safe space where they can share their deepest emotions that they think others don't want to hear.
